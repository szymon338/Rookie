How to win projects - in a nutshell

1. Search for projects in the project directory

2. Look for projects that are relevant to your skills

3. Place a bid - write the whole thing nice and custom like. Think about what would make you hire someone

4. If a client messages you, in response to your bid, explain to them how great you are at whatever you do, show them samples, and then tell them to hire you!

5. Profit
